NEW CLUB MEMBERS

Red Beach Outrigger Canoe Club (RBOCC) has a trial period for all “new paddlers” where they have 4 paddling sessions to decide whether they like the club and sport of Waka Ama (Outrigger Canoe Paddling) before they are required to join the club to continue paddling.

ID CARDS

WANZ ID registration cards have a fee of $5.00 per paddler. (These fees are subject to change, especially at peak time, close to the Sprint Nationals where prices may increase). These cards are a must to be able to compete in any of the Waka Ama regattas. (No ID card, no paddle). An electronic copy of the items below must be sent to: info@rbocc.co.nz
1. Submit a headshot photo
2. Provide a copy of proof of ID (copy of passport preferred, if not copy of drivers license or birth certificate)

EXISTING MEMBERS

For existing club members club fees are due on the anniversary of their club membership to be paid via the clubs online portal. All membership payments are to be done through the clubs portal and any forms are to be returned to the Club Treasurer.

CLUB PORTAL

Red Beach Waka Ama Club expects all registered members to commit a certain amount of time and dedication towards activities that the club organizes and involves itself in. As a member of the club your responsibilities are to:

  • Foster the visions, values and objectives of the club
  • Maintain the integrity of the Club at all times
  • Commit to your team and be a team player
  • Treat all of the clubs equipment with care and report any damages as per the appropriate guidelines
  • Treat all members of the club with courtesy and respect
  • Participate in social hui events
  • Assist in fundraising activities
  • Pay membership fees on time
