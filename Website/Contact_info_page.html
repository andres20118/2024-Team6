FAQ

Who are we?

We are a outrigger canoe club devoted to bringing Waka Ama into mainstream sports while providing a welcoming community for people of all ages to stay physically fit! Find more about us on the About Us Page.

Is this sport safe?

Like all sports, 100% safety is not guarenteed, however, we have multiple procedures, educators, and guidlines in place to ensure that everyone who canoes' with us feels safe and comfortable. You can find how important we value safety Here.
