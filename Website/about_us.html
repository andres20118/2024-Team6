Red Beach Outrigger Canoe Club

is a young up and coming Waka Ama club that was established in 2018 to lead the development and practice of Waka Ama, With a key focus to promote kaupapa waka as a positive activity to support cultural and physical wellbeing within the community.

Waka Ama has proven to be a growing team sport across New Zealand, The sport presents a rich history where identity, tradition and community is at its core and as a result, for many, Waka Ama is not just a sport but also a lifestyle.

Constitution

Red Beach Outrigger Canoe Club was established in 2018 and is situated in Red Beach Whangaparoa, Auckland New Zealand. Red

Beach Outrigger Canoe Club has been established to assist and implement the objects of Waka Ama NZ within the Auckland Region.

The Constitution of the Red Beach Outrigger Canoe Club Incorporated comprehensively outlines core provisions, members, governance, general meetings, elections and voting, along with other related matters.
